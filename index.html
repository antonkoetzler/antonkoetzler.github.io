About Me

Hello, I'm Anton Koetzler-Faust. I'm a 20-year-old university student attending the University of Lethbridge. I'm from Calgary, however I'm half Brazilian. Ever since my university enrollment, I've learned lots about specifically C++ and web development. Always having a fascination for coding since I was a child, I've grown to love it mainly due to the creativity and endless innovation it offers. More specifically, I find that my drive/motivation for programming comes from creating technologies that dogmatically follow software minimalism, and strive to have the user actually understand the piece of technology intricately or to an intermediate level. Fortunately, I think this is the future of programming due to a rapidly smarter population, and the essentiality of computing in general.
